Week 1 Tasks & Checklist
  • ☐ Choose brinjal variety: Thai, Japanese, or Indian long varieties
  • ☐ Select hottest spot in garden (full sun, 6-8 hours)
  • ☐ Test soil pH (ideal: 5.5-6.5)
  • ☐ Add plenty of compost and aged manure (eggplants are heavy feeders)
  • ☐ Ensure soil temperature is at least 21°C before planting
  • ☐ Plan spacing: 60-75cm apart for good airflow
  • ☐ Prepare black plastic mulch to warm soil (optional but recommended)
Pro Tip: Eggplants LOVE heat. If you're in a cooler climate, start seeds indoors 8-10 weeks before last frost and use black plastic mulch to warm the soil!

📋 Week 2 Tasks Preview
  • 🌱 Start seeds indoors 8-10 weeks before transplant
  • 🌡️ Maintain soil temperature at 24-29°C for best germination
  • 💧 Keep soil consistently moist
  • ☀️ Provide 14-16 hours of light
  • 🌿 Transplant to larger pots after 2-3 true leaves appear
