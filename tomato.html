Week 1: Variety Selection & Site Prep Available now
Week 1 Tasks & Checklist
  • ☐ Choose tomato variety: Determinate (bush) vs Indeterminate (vining)
  • ☐ Select a sunny spot with 6-8 hours of direct sunlight
  • ☐ Test soil pH (ideal: 6.0-6.8)
  • ☐ Add compost and aged manure to enrich soil
  • ☐ Prepare support structures: cages, stakes, or trellis
  • ☐ Plan spacing: 45-60cm apart for good airflow
  • ☐ Clear area of weeds and debris
  • ☐ Water soil deeply and let settle for 2-3 days before planting
Pro Tip: Heirloom varieties have amazing flavor but less disease resistance. Cherry tomatoes are easiest for beginners!
Week 2: Seed Starting & Germination Complete Week 1 to unlock

Video: Starting tomato seeds indoors (locked)

📋 Week 2 Tasks (Preview)
  • 🌱 Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before last frost
  • 🌡️ Maintain soil temperature at 21-27°C for germination
  • 💧 Keep soil moist but not waterlogged
  • ☀️ Provide 14-16 hours of light using grow lights
  • 🌿 Thin seedlings to one per cell after true leaves appear
  • 🔄 Rotate trays daily for even growth
Week 3: Transplanting & Staking Unlocks after Week 2
📋 Week 3 Tasks Preview
  • ✔️ Harden off seedlings over 7-10 days
  • ✔️ Transplant after last frost when soil is warm
  • ✔️ Plant deeper than container (tomatoes root from stem)
  • ✔️ Install stakes or cages immediately after planting
  • ✔️ Water deeply with compost tea
  • ✔️ Apply mulch to retain moisture
