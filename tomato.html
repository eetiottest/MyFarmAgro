Course Modules 📅 Weekly releases
Week 1: Site Selection & Soil Preparation Available now
Week 1 Tasks
  • Choose a sunny location with 6-8 hours of direct sunlight
  • Test soil pH (ideal: 6.0-6.8)
  • Clear the area of weeds, rocks, and debris
  • Mix compost into soil (30% compost, 70% topsoil)
  • Prepare raised beds or containers with drainage holes
  • Water the soil thoroughly and let it settle for 2-3 days
Pro Tip: Tomatoes love warm soil. Add eggshells to prevent blossom end rot.
Week 2: Seed Starting & Germination Releases in 7 days
📋 Week 2 Tasks
  • Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before last frost
  • Maintain soil temperature at 21-27°C
  • Provide 14-16 hours of light using grow lights
  • Keep soil moist but not waterlogged
