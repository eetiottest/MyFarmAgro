Week 1: Site Selection & Soil Preparation Available now
Week 1 Tasks & Checklist
  • Choose a sunny location with 6-8 hours of direct sunlight
  • Test soil pH using a home testing kit (ideal: 6.0-6.8)
  • Clear the area of weeds, rocks, and debris
  • Mix compost into soil (30% compost, 70% topsoil ratio)
  • Prepare raised beds or containers with drainage holes
  • Water the soil thoroughly and let it settle for 2-3 days
  • Gather seeds: choose between local varieties or hybrid seeds
  • Create a planting calendar marking key dates
Pro Tip: Chili plants love warmth. If you're in a cooler climate, start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before the last frost date.
Week 2: Seed Starting & Germination Releases in 7 days
Week 2 Tasks
  • Soak seeds in warm water for 8 hours before planting
  • Fill seed trays with seed-starting mix
  • Plant seeds 0.5cm deep, cover lightly with soil
  • Maintain temperature between 25-30°C for germination
  • Keep soil moist but not waterlogged
  • Provide 14-16 hours of light using grow lights
Week 3: Transplanting & Early Care Releases in 14 days
📋 Week 3 Tasks
  • Harden off seedlings over 7-10 days
  • Transplant when seedlings have 4-6 true leaves
  • Space plants 45-60cm apart for good airflow
  • Apply mulch around plants to retain moisture
  • Water deeply after transplanting
